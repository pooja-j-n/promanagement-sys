Batch formation

Do students have freedom to choose their partners?
Yes, even interdisciplinary projects are allowed.
Are industry projects allowed?
Project may be perfomed In-house/Industry/R&D Institution
How many members in the group?
Depends on the number specified by the committee in the list of projects uploaded.
What decides the number of members?
The complexity of the project and features expected from the project.

Topic Selection

What type of topics are expected?
Pertinent to the courseload and based on cutting edge technologies in the market.
How cutting edge?
Not much research must be done on the topic. Check up research papers dating not less than a year prior to submission of synopsis.
Does the project committee give topics?
Yes, mainly they are given by the committee. If students wish to do a project of their own, they must submit a write up for review by the committee.
How long before committee gives approval?
A week.
